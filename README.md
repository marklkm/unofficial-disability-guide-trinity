# The Unofficial Disability Guide to Trinity College Dublin

The Unofficial Disability Guide to Trinity is a student-facing guide created by students with disabilities, for students with disabilities.

It brings together practical information, student knowledge, lived experience and links to support for disabled and neurodivergent students at Trinity College Dublin.

The guide is designed to help students know where to start, what to ask, and where to find support. It recognises that disability can be visible, invisible, physical, sensory, neurodivergent, mental health-related, chronic, fluctuating, temporary, diagnosed or undiagnosed.

This is a living guide shaped by student experience, disability community, accessibility, belonging and the belief that disabled students should not have to search alone for clear information.

Support. Belonging. Clear information. You are not alone.
